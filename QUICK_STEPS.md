# Quick Steps to Deploy Rules

## You're almost there! Just need to deploy the security rules.

### Step 1: Navigate to Rules Tab
In your Firebase Console (where you currently are):
1. Look for the "Rules" tab at the top (next to "Data", "Indexes", etc.)
2. Click on "Rules"

### Step 2: Copy the Rules
Open the file `firestore.rules` in your project and copy ALL the content

### Step 3: Paste and Publish
1. Paste the rules into the Firebase Console editor
2. Click "Publish" button
3. Wait 30 seconds

### Step 4: Test Login
Go to http://localhost:5174/login and login with:
- Email: niinortey@n-qube.com
- Password: [your password]

That's it!
